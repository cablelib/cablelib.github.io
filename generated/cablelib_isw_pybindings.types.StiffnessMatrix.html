Calculates the stiffness matrix.

The stiffness matrix is the sum of the geometrical and linear-elastic stiffness matrices. It is denoted as K_{OS} in Pott, Andreas. Cable-Driven Parallel Robots (2017). Refer to chapter 3.8.2
